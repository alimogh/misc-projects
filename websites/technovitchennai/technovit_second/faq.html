Frequently Asked Questions.

Q) What is Qubit, Connectivitieee, Desino and VITness, Tai:kuun and Glitz ?
Qubit – Name for the Technical/Nontechnical and Gaming events conducted by School of Computing Science and Engineering (SCSE)
Connectivitee - Name for the Technical/Nontechnical and Gaming events conducted by School of Electrical and Electronics Engineering & Electronics and Communication Engineering (SELECT)
Desino - Name for the Technical/Nontechnical and Gaming events conducted by School of Mechanical and Building Sciences (SMBS)
VITness - Name for the Technical/Nontechnical and Gaming events conducted by Law School (LS)
Tai:kuun - Name for the Technical/Nontechnical and Gaming events conducted by Business School (BS)
Glitz – Symposia with Technical/Nontechnical and Gaming events conducted by VIT fashion Technology School (VFIT)

Q) What is the payment procedure?
  1. In the technoVIT website, there is a registration tab which redirects you to an online registration portal called eventz’e
  2. You can create your account and register for any number of events or workshops and can also order t-shirts.
  3. Once you confirm, we will get your details. When you come to the campus, head towards the registration desk and pay the total amount and get the verification.
  4. An Invoice will be sent to your email id with which you can use to participate in the respective event(s)/workshop(s).

Q) What is the transportation facility provided by/near the VIT Chennai Campus?
Participants are requested to make use of their own transport services. But if there are more than 50 students from a single institution, options might be arranged for transportation.

Q) What is the accommodation procedure?
When a participant needs accommodation, they need to plan well in advance and inform the event coordinators(refer the website for the event pages) about their duration of stay. The event coordinators would make the accommodation arrangements at a specified cost of Rs. 450/day with food. However if there are more than 25 registrations from any institution as a whole, they would be given free accommodation provided if the event coordinator arranged the information already.

Q) Will there be any food facility provided within the VIT Chennai Campus?
Yes! We have several food stalls both North Indian and South Indian where you would have delicious food, beverages, desserts to satisfy your hunger and quench your thirst.

Q) What should I do after coming to the VITC Campus?
As soon as you reach the campus, head towards the registration desk located at the Academic Block entrance and verify with your event and payment details.

Q) Do I need to carry my university ID card?
Carrying of official college issued Identity Card is mandatory for all personnel visiting the campus for technoVIT16. The ID is also required for desk registrations for all events.

Q) Are there any ATMs within/nearby the campus?
There are two ATM Machines available in the campus premises, one near the Campus Health Center (Karur Vysya Bank ATM) and the other located near VMart Convenience Store (Indian Bank ATM). Also Axis Bank and HDFC Bank ATMs are located within 50 meters outside campus premises.

Q) Is there any health center inside the university campus?
A health center with emergency facilities and ambulance service is available 24/7 inside the campus.

Q) What if I make a mistake while filling my details on the online registration portal?
You will have to contact the admin for the changes that you want to make in your profile before your event/workshop starts. You can also do that at the time of making the payment at the registration desk.
