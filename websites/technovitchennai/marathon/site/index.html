About Marathon

Theme: “I Pledge to Give way for Ambulance”


TECHNOVIT Marathon promotes the techno-management fest organized by the students of VIT University-Chennai and is going to have its 1st edition on 15th October 2015.
"GIVE WAY TO AMBULANCE" continues to be the Technovit Marathon theme. We believe the theme resonates with all the runners & inspires each runner to the finish line.
Give way to ambulance theme is based on the app “give way to ambulance”. This app is used to give an alert to surrounding people to make sure that they don’t block the road.
Socially Responsible organizations around the world are promoting 'Running' to create health awareness and India is catching up fast. There are more than 250 marathons are being organized in India alone and these numbers are growing rapidly. Some advanced cities like Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, etc. host more than a marathon every month!
